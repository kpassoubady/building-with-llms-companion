# Billing FAQ

## When is my invoice generated?

Invoices are generated on the first day of each month for the previous month's
usage. You will receive an email notification with a link to the invoice PDF in
your account dashboard.

## What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express credit cards. ACH bank transfer
is available for accounts on annual plans. Invoiced billing (net-30 terms) is
available for enterprise accounts with a signed contract.

## How do I update my payment method?

Go to Settings > Billing > Payment Methods. You can add a new card and set it
as the default. Old cards can be removed once they are no longer attached to an
active subscription.

## What happens if a payment fails?

If a payment fails, we retry automatically on days 3, 7, and 14 after the
original due date. You will receive an email notification after each failed
attempt. If payment is not received by day 14, the account is suspended. Data
is retained for 30 days after suspension before permanent deletion.

## Can I get a refund?

Refunds are available within 7 days of purchase for annual plans if the service
has not been used. Monthly plan charges are non-refundable. Contact support at
billing@example.com with your account ID and the reason for the refund request.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to Settings > Billing > Subscription and click Cancel Plan. Cancellation
takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You will retain access
until that date and will not be charged again.
