# Employee Onboarding Guide

## Your First Week

Welcome to the team. Your first week is focused on getting set up and meeting
your colleagues. Your manager will schedule a series of introductory meetings
across your first three days.

On day one, IT will provision your laptop, email account, and access to the
internal tools you need. Bring a government-issued ID for the security badge.

## Required Training

All new employees must complete the following training within the first 30 days:

- Security awareness: covers phishing, password policy, and data handling
- Code of conduct: reviews expected workplace behaviour and reporting channels
- Data privacy: covers GDPR obligations and how to handle customer data

Training is completed online through the learning portal. Your manager receives
a notification once you finish each module.

## Access and Accounts

Submit access requests through the IT ticketing system. Standard turnaround is
one business day for software access and up to three days for elevated permissions.
Your manager must approve requests for production system access.

## Expense Reimbursement

Submit expense reports within 30 days of incurring a cost. Attach receipts for
any item over $25. Reimbursements are processed in the next payroll cycle after
approval. Meals during business travel are capped at $75 per day.
