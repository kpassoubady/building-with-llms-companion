# Capstone 2: Global Intelligent Fleet Assistant

---

## The Goal
Build a robust, production-ready conversational agent tailored to fleet management and driver support. 

You will apply advanced techniques beyond basic prompt engineering.

---

## What We Are Building

1. **RAG Pipeline**: Answer questions using official fleet policy documents to prevent hallucinations.
2. **Safe Multi-Turn Chat**: A stateful chatbot equipped with guardrails to block off-topic queries and dangerous advice.
3. **Automated Evaluation**: A testing script to measure the accuracy of an LLM extracting data from messy invoices.

---

## Grading Rubric (Self-Assessment)

- **Exercise 1 (RAG)**: Does the model accurately cite the policy instead of its training data?
- **Exercise 2 (Guardrails)**: Does the bot gracefully decline to answer non-automotive questions? Does it remember context?
- **Exercise 3 (Evaluation)**: Does the script correctly parse the dataset and output a pass/fail accuracy metric?
