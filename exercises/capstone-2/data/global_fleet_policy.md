# Global Fleet Management - Driver Policy Guidelines

## 1. Preventative Maintenance Schedule
- **Oil Changes**: Required every 7,500 miles or 6 months (whichever comes first) for standard internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles. Fully synthetic oil must be used.
- **Tire Rotation**: Recommended every 10,000 miles to ensure even tread wear.
- **Brake Inspection**: Mandatory annual inspection for all fleet vehicles. Pads must be replaced if thickness is below 3mm.
- **EV Battery Check**: For electric vehicles in the fleet, a battery health diagnostic must be performed every 12 months.

## 2. Maintenance Approval & Limits
- **Pre-Approved Repairs**: Routine maintenance (oil changes, tire rotations, wiper blades) under $150 does not require pre-approval.
- **Approval Required**: Any repair exceeding $150 must be submitted to the Global Fleet Service Center for authorization prior to work commencing.
- **Unauthorized Work**: Drivers are strictly prohibited from performing DIY repairs on fleet vehicles. Doing so voids the warranty and violates fleet policy.

## 3. Accident & Emergency Procedures
- In the event of an accident, ensure safety first and call emergency services if required.
- Contact the Global Emergency Assistance line at 1-800-555-0199 immediately.
- Do not admit fault at the scene. Collect insurance info and take photos.
- Towing is covered up to 50 miles to the nearest authorized repair facility.

## 4. Mileage Reporting
- Personal mileage must be reported by the 5th of every month using the Global Driver App.
- Failure to report personal mileage will result in an assumed 100% business use, which may lead to tax implications for the driver.
